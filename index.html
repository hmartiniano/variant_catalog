Genetic Variant Lookup

FH-related variant catalog

This resource was developed as part of the PerMedFH project to enhance the diagnosis of familial hypercholesterolemia (FH) and support personalized treatment by sharing data from functional studies on FH variants. Previously published variants are also included, and the database will be regularly updated with new findings from the PerMedFH project. Last update: June 2025
